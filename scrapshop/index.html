About Us

RTHT Recycling Pte Ltd, incorporated in Singapore , is a premier provider of collection, trading, and recycling services for all grades of ferrous and non-ferrous metals, industrial scraps, and electronic waste. Additionally, we supply various types of industrial machinery and equipment. As one of Singapore's leading Metal Waste Collectors and Scrap Metal Buyers, RTHT Recycling offers the best scrap metal prices in the region. Sell your scrap metal to us at competitive rates. Our diverse customer base includes homes, schools, commercial buildings, factories, warehouses, construction sites, shipyards, and more. We primarily resell metal scrap to the local market and also deal in industrial machinery and equipment, selling usable items locally and to parts of Malaysia, Indonesia, and India. While we connect buyers and sellers within Singapore's metal scrap industry, we also facilitate international purchases from established scrap recycling companies in Singapore.
